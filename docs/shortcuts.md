## Customization

Shortcuts can be customized in the preferences:

1. Open Preferences (Ctrl+,)
2. Navigate to Keyboard Shortcuts
3. Click on a shortcut to reassign
4. Press the new key combination
5. Click Apply

### Conflict Resolution

If a shortcut conflicts:
- Panel-specific shortcuts take precedence
- Global shortcuts apply when no panel is focused
- Warnings shown for conflicts
